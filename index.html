Our mission is to protect everyone’s right to free from the threat of sexual exploitation and trafficking.

We exist to offer hope for a new life to people who are involved in commercial sexual exploitation (CSE), prostitution, and sex trafficking. The mission is to provide needed services to survivors of CSE and sex trafficking including immediate shelter, education, job skills training, and long-term safe houses.

Sex trafficking is a form of modern slary when victims are pushed to performing sex acts. It is human trafficking for the purpose of exploit victims sexually. The perperators, pimps and traffickers, would usually control victims emotionally and physically. They might use verbal abuse, physical abuse, control victims using drugs, or threatening them with other tools to traffick victims and profit from forcing them into practice commercial sex with pay customers. These victims usually find it hard to escape from this life style because the pimps usually take away the majority amount of the money they make. The victims would also find themselves growing dependent on the traffickers emotionally and financially. Any kind of sexually exploitation of adults and children are illegal. This includes acquisition of individuals, transporting and exploitation of victims. There are a lot of ways you as normal citizens can do to help. Check out our Get Involved page to take actions to help fight human trafficking!
